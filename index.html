Let's show bed bugs who's boss in our city.

Bite Back

Chicago has been ranked the bed bug capital of America for 4 of the last 5 years.
Bed bugs don't discriminate: they can affect anyone, from CEOs to minimum-wage workers. Infestations can lead to incredible mental stress and fatigue, and has even been linked to depression. At the same time, some of Chicago's most at risk people are most commonly affected by bed bugs. You can help get infestation-preventing resources to those who need it most.
Bite Back provides resources on bed bug prevention and removal to individuals in Chicago's affordable housing facilities, so that they can focus on building a solid foundation for their lives.
